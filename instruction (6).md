# **Phantom & Co. \- Professional Haunting Simulator**

# **Project Title**

Phantom & Co. \- Professional Haunting Simulator

---

# **Project Context**

Phantom & Co. is a paranormal agency that provides professional haunting services for clients seeking supernatural experiences. The player assumes the role of a ghost employed by the agency and completes haunting contracts by frightening AI-controlled human characters inside haunted buildings.

The objective is to develop a browser-based reverse horror game that allows players to complete haunting missions using supernatural abilities while progressing through increasingly challenging levels.

---

# **Project Goal**

Develop a fully playable browser-based game where players control a ghost, complete haunting missions, increase the fear level of AI-controlled human characters, and progress through three sequential missions.

---

# **Target Platform**

The game shall run entirely inside desktop web browsers.

The game shall not require installation of additional software.

The game shall support the latest stable versions of:

* Google Chrome  
* Microsoft Edge  
* Mozilla Firefox

---

# **Gameplay Requirements**

## **Player Character**

The player shall control a ghost during every mission.

The ghost shall remain the playable character throughout the game.

---

## **Haunted Environments**

The game shall contain the following three haunted environments:

* Abandoned House  
* Haunted Manor  
* Old Museum

Each environment shall contain one mission.

Players shall be able to move throughout the active environment.

---

## **Ghost Abilities**

The player shall be able to activate the following ghost abilities independently:

* Flicker lights  
* Move objects  
* Open doors  
* Whisper  
* Trigger paranormal events

Each ability shall be available during gameplay.

---

## **Human Characters**

Each mission shall include AI-controlled human characters.

Human characters shall react to ghost abilities by increasing their fear level.

---

# **Fear Meter**

A Fear Meter shall remain visible during gameplay.

The Fear Meter shall increase when ghost abilities successfully affect AI-controlled human characters.

Each mission shall require the Fear Meter to reach its target value before the mission can be completed.

---

# **Mission System**

The game shall contain three missions.

Each mission shall include:

* one mission objective  
* one victory condition  
* one failure condition

Completing a mission shall unlock the next mission.

---

# **Scoring System**

The game shall display the current score during gameplay.

The score shall increase after successful completion of mission objectives.

The final score shall be displayed on the victory screen.

---

# **Game Progression**

The game shall contain three sequential levels.

Level 2 shall present a greater challenge than Level 1\.

Level 3 shall present a greater challenge than Level 2\.

The increased challenge shall be achieved by introducing additional AI-controlled human characters or requiring a higher Fear Meter target.

---

# **User Interface**

The game shall include the following screens:

* Main Menu  
* Gameplay Screen  
* Victory Screen  
* Failure Screen

The Gameplay Screen shall display:

* Fear Meter  
* Current Score  
* Current Mission

---

# **Audio**

The game shall include sound effects for:

* Flickering lights  
* Whisper ability  
* Door interaction  
* Paranormal event activation  
* Mission completion

---

# **Animation**

The game shall include animations for:

* Ghost movement  
* Light flickering  
* Object movement  
* Door opening  
* Paranormal event activation

---

# **Responsive Layout**

The game interface shall remain usable after resizing the browser window.

Gameplay controls and interface elements shall remain accessible after browser resizing.

---

# **Browser Compatibility**

The game shall function correctly in:

* Google Chrome (latest stable version)  
* Microsoft Edge (latest stable version)  
* Mozilla Firefox (latest stable version)

---

# **Deliverables**

The completed project shall include:

1. Complete website source code.  
2. All game assets required to run the game.  
3. One playable browser game.  
4. One revision round for minor gameplay or interface adjustments.

---

# **Scope Boundaries**

The project does not include:

* Multiplayer gameplay  
* User accounts  
* Online leaderboards  
* Cloud saves  
* Backend services  
* Mobile application development  
* Downloadable content  
* In-game purchases

---
